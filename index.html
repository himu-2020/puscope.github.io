about us

We have build this for Educational purpose only For Students specially.

We are a team of students who are working on this project to provide a platform for students to share their knowledge and knowledge of other students.

Student can seek help

Complaint

Students can complain about any issue related to studies or any other issue. Also they can complain about any issue related to the website, And we will try to solve it as soon as possible. We believe in safety of students so feel free to reach us out.

Support

If you are facing any issue related to studies or any other issue, contact us on the support portal. our support team will help you out.

Report

If any student is found to be misbehaving or doing any illegal work on the website, then you can report him/her to us. We will take strict action against him/her.
